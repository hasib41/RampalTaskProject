Project Overview

The Maitree Super Thermal Power Project is a landmark initiative in the energy sector of Bangladesh. Developed as a 1320 MW coal-fired power station, it represents the strategic partnership between Bangladesh and India to ensure energy security for the region.

Equipped with state-of-the-art Ultra-Supercritical technology, the plant is designed to operate with significantly higher efficiency than traditional plants, resulting in lower fuel consumption and reduced carbon footprint. It serves as a cornerstone for industrial growth in the Khulna division and contributes substantially to the national grid.
