General Instructions to Bidders

Bidders are requested to carefully review the following instructions before submitting their proposals through the e-GP portal.

  • All tenders must be submitted electronically through the National e-Government Procurement (e-GP) System portal.
  • The tender security must be submitted in the form of a Bank Guarantee from any scheduled bank of Bangladesh.
  • A non-refundable tender document fee must be paid for each tender as specified in the tender notice.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted by the electronic system under any circumstances.
  • BIFPCL reserves the right to accept or reject any or all bids without assigning any reason whatsoever.

Important Note: Pre-bid meetings for all active tenders are held every Wednesday at 10:00 AM in the BIFPCL Conference Room or via Microsoft Teams.
